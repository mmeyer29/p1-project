Things occasionally don't do what I expect them too, which causes some delays. However, when I isolate and investigate these paranormal phenomena, I ultimately gain a deeper understanding of CSS, which is overall a very positive thing.

I'm having a very difficult time targeting what I want to target within CSS, although I do think I'm finally startign to grasp the concept of parents and children as it pertains to the flexbox. I will keep practicing though.
